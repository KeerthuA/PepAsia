‘ PEP ASIA ‘ .... WOK and GRILL , is the new kid on the block offering takeaway food which is predominantly Malaysian , Chinese and Thai food .
I can’t be happier as Chef Arvind Raja is someone for whom I have huge respect for his immense knowledge
on South East Asian Cuisine . I was so very impressed with the way the menu
has been curated ! Absolutely interesting and unique .
Grills had Grilled Spicy Fresh Chilli Jumbo Prawns
which has been marinated and grilled to perfection .
Mala Chicken skewers is an impressive innovation .
Soto Ayam was terrific with a boiled egg
added to the soup which made it seem like a wholesome
meal by itself and the broth had clear flavours !
Kicap Chilli Chicken simply superb !
Chef Arvind s play with unique flavours
makes the dish come alive .
Mushroom Mapo Tofu yet another stunner .
Nasi Lemak with fried
chicken wings was packed so compactly
that it took me back to Sarawak Islands where I
have learnt to make this dish .
The fried chicken wings were nice and plump unlike
scrawny wings given in takeways at times and the highlight
was the Anchovy sauce which was finger licking good .
Roasted peanuts and a boiled egg made the Nasi Lemak a winner straightaway .
Kway Teow was authentic with the flat noodles tossed perfectly ,
Thai Chilli Basil fried rice nice and spicy !
SOP followed stringently and all dishes neatly packed .
Thanks Chef Arvind for this fabulous South East Asian meal
and my best wishes to you and Team Pep Asia !
I’m sure PEP ASIA will rock the city soon.
GOD BLESS .....
